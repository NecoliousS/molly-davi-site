Pre-Game Ritual

THE DUTCH BRAID

Before every game, Molly does two braids into a bun — a Dutch braid. It's her signature look, her mental reset, her way of locking in. By the time the braids are done, she's ready to deal.

The Moment

THE TURNING POINT

Before freshman year, Molly discovered her true talent and ability. Competing at a national tournament, she finished in the top 10 — going up against players already committed to Division 1 programs.

That was the moment. The realization that she wasn't just good — she was D1 capable. That she belonged on the biggest stage.

From that day forward, everything changed. The training got harder. The focus got sharper. The dream got real.
